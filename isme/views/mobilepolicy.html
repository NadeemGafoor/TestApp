Dine around the Emirates with your exclusive IHG ® Dining Rewards Mobile App. You can download the application to your iPhone to manage your IHG ® Dining Rewards and benefits. You can activate offers and use them at the listed offers by signing in to the app using your IHG® Dining Rewards membership ID and app password. No other personal identifiable information will be requested by IHG® Dining Rewards through the Mobile App. You can enable or disable the following services 1) IHG® Dining Rewards Notification Services. 2) Location Services.

    If you have downloaded the application to your iPhone, you can take advantage of finding a Restaurant near you through the Global Position System (GPS) technology. In addition, IHG ® Dining Rewards will send you real-time offers and experiences nearest to you through the Geo-Fence technology. No personally identifiable information is retained to deliver this services. If you do not wish to receive these offers, you can disable Push Notification Services under Settings Menu.
    Battery Disclaimer: Continued use of GPS running in the background may decrease battery life.
